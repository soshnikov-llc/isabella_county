Methodology. ROI ratios are drawn from peer-reviewed meta-analyses and systematic reviews. Isabella County–specific estimates apply published ROI ratios to local population subgroups derived from the disease burden analysis (see dashboard). Estimates assume published program effectiveness can be replicated in a rural Michigan setting, which may require adjustment for local context. Use sliders to model different program reach scenarios. All figures are in 2024–2026 US dollars. "Savings" include direct medical cost reductions, averted hospitalizations, and — under the societal perspective — productivity gains and criminal justice savings. These are planning estimates, not audited projections.
