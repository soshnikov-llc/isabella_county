Methodology (June 2026 v5.0 update). ROI ratios are recalculated from Cochrane and peer-reviewed meta-analyses (Reilly 2024 for MH; Perry 2025 for MAT; Whittaker 2019 for tobacco; Agarwal 2025 for BP; Freeman 2021 for cancer screening; Jiang 2026 for diabetes). Formula: DALYs averted = target DALYs × programme reach × meta-analysis effect fraction; savings = DALYs averted × $62,000 (Michigan human capital rate, GDP per capita 2024). ROI = savings ÷ annualised investment. Estimates apply to Isabella County population subgroups derived from CDC PLACES 2024; SUD and CVD prevalence are derived, and several mortality inputs are rural-adjusted proxies. MH and cancer screening ROI are below 2:1, reflecting limitations of cited evidence. All figures ±30–50% uncertainty - directional planning estimates only, not audited projections. Use sliders to model different reach scenarios.
Population base: 64,565 total / ~52,297 adults · Primary burden: 15,430 DALYs (frontier LE + MH remission-adj; 95% UI 14,459–16,481) · Planning sensitivity (MI LE 78.6 yrs): 10,579 DALYs · Monte Carlo n=10,000 · Full methods →
